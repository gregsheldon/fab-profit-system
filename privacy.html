4. Calculator And Report Data

The FabProfit Baseline Efficiency Calculator is designed to generate a planning report based on information you enter. The report may include modeled exposure, estimating hours, known-loss floor, process maturity score, recoverable value, and payback-style calculations.

The calculator may store information in your browser through sessionStorage or localStorage so the report page can display your numbers. It may also pass report information to the report page through a browser URL hash. A URL hash is normally not sent to the web server by the browser, but it can still be visible to anyone who has access to your browser, device, screenshots, or copied report link.

If an opt-in form, CRM, email platform, automation, or endpoint is connected, information submitted through the form may also be sent to that service so the report can be delivered, saved, followed up on, or used for a company license discussion.

Do not share a generated report link publicly if the report contains company-sensitive numbers. Clear your browser storage if you do not want the report data saved locally on that device.
